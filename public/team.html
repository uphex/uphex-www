John Feminella, co-founder

John Feminella is co-founder at UpHex, his fifth startup. John is an avid technologist, occasional public speaker, and frequent instigator of assorted shenanigans. He guest lectures at the University of Virginia and mentors budding entrepreneurs at HackCville.

John attended the University of Virginia, where he was an Echols Scholar and a Rodman Scholar. He lives in Charlottesville, VA.

Bradley Kipp, co-founder

Bradley Kipp is co-founder at UpHex, his second startup (ad)venture. Previously, Bradley was a founding VP at Cardagin Networks, where he worked alongside John for two years. Before the startup world, Bradley was a corporate and investment banker.
