What is UpHex?
UpHex monitors your analytics metrics from across the web. After connecting your accounts, we monitor your metrics in the background and alert you when there’s interest or unusual activity. UpHex currently works with Google Analytics, Facebook, Twitter, and Shopify.
What is “Proactive Alerting”?
“Proactive Alerting” means we do everything for you. UpHex is designed to provide relevant information without you needing to search for answers or constantly check dashboards. It’s completely automatic! Meaning you can focus on your job; we’ll look after your metrics.
Who is UpHex?
UpHex is a small team dedicated to making analytics easier for everyone. The company was co-founded by John Feminella and Bradley Kipp in 2013.
I have a question / comment / suggestion. Can I contact you?
Absolutely! We’d love to hear from you. You can email us at support@uphex.com. We’ll respond to all inquiries within one business day.
UpHex sounds great! How do I get started?
Click here to sign up for an account. Once you’ve signed up, simply connect your Google Analytics, Facebook, Twitter, and/or Shopify accounts. Then sit back and relax - we’ll alert you when we notice unusual activity in one of your accounts.
What information do I need to give at sign up?
All you need is an email address and password. Signing up and connecting your accounts takes less than 5 minutes!
I manage multiple Google Analytics properties. Which one(s) will be connected?
We let you pick the Google Analytics properties you want to connect. After you authorize UpHex to connect with your Google Analytics account, we provide a list of all your Google Analytics properties. Just check the ones you want to add then click the “Add” button.
How many accounts can I connect?
As many as you like! We don’t limit the number of accounts you can connect to UpHex.
What services does UpHex integrate with?
We currently integrate with Google Analytics, Facebook, Twitter, and Shopify.
I use a service that’s not listed. What should I do?
Let us know! We’re working to add more integrations and would love suggestions from you. Shoot us an email at support@uphex.com.
